About Qube Money

Qube Money takes the traditional cash envelope method and brings it into the digital age. Unlike other apps that simply track what you've already spent, Qube Money changes your spending behavior at the point of sale. By requiring you to choose a "Qube" (category) before making a purchase, it creates a moment of mindfulness that prevents overspending before it happens.

The platform combines a mobile banking app with a specialized debit card. Your money is held in a "Cloud" account, and funds are only transferred to your card when you intentionally open a Qube. This proactive approach ensures you always stay within your budget and live within your means.

Key Features

  • Digital Envelopes (Qubes): Organize your money into specific digital buckets for every expense
  • Proactive Spending Control: You must select a category in the app before your card will authorize a transaction
  • Real-Time Balances: See exactly how much is left in a specific category instantly after a purchase
  • Automatic Tracking: No more manual entry; every transaction is automatically categorized to its Qube
  • Partner & Family Accounts: Sync budgets with a partner or manage "Kid Qubes" for financial education
  • Subscription Management: Create dedicated Qubes for recurring bills to ensure they are always covered
  • Debt Reduction Tools: Allocate funds specifically toward paying down balances
  • FDIC Insured: Banking services are provided through partner banks, ensuring your deposits are protected

Who Should Use Qube Money?

Qube Money is perfect for "proactive budgeters"—people who want to prevent overspending rather than just track it after the fact. It is an excellent solution for couples who struggle with financial communication, as it provides a single source of truth for shared categories. It's also ideal for parents who want to teach their children mindful spending habits using a digital-first approach.
