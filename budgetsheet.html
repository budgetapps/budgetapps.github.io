About BudgetSheet

BudgetSheet is a powerful extension for Google Sheets that bridges the gap between modern financial automation and the total control of a spreadsheet. It allows you to link your real-world bank accounts, credit cards, and investment accounts directly to your own custom spreadsheets.

Instead of being locked into a rigid app interface, BudgetSheet gives you the raw data you need to build your own custom dashboards, pivot tables, and financial models. It eliminates the tedious task of manual transaction entry while keeping you in the driver's seat of your financial analysis.

Key Features

  • Direct Bank Integration: Securely link over 12,000 financial institutions via Plaid
  • Automated Imports: Fetch new transactions and balances with a single click or on a schedule
  • Total Flexibility: Use all the power of Google Sheets (formulas, charts, and scripts) on your real data
  • Auto-Categorization: Automatically categorize transactions based on your own custom rules
  • Multi-Currency Support: Handle accounts and transactions in various global currencies
  • Historical Data: Import months of historical transactions to start your analysis immediately
  • Privacy Focused: Your data lives in your Google Sheet, not on a third-party budgeting server
  • No Subscription Lock-in: Own your data and your templates forever

Who Should Use BudgetSheet?

BudgetSheet is designed for the "spreadsheet power user." If you find traditional budgeting apps too restrictive or frustrating, and you find yourself constantly exporting CSVs to do your own analysis, BudgetSheet is the perfect solution. It's ideal for those who want the automation of a modern fintech app combined with the infinite customization of Google Sheets.
