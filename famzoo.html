About FamZoo

FamZoo is an award-winning virtual family bank designed to help parents teach their children the fundamentals of earning, saving, spending, and giving. By providing a safe, controlled environment, FamZoo allows kids to learn from their financial mistakes while the stakes are still low. The system works like a "private family bank" where the parents act as the bankers and the children are the customers.

Unlike traditional banking apps, FamZoo focuses heavily on education and automation. Parents can set up automated allowances, tie rewards to chores, and even charge "parent-paid interest" on savings to demonstrate the power of compound growth. Whether you use their physical prepaid cards or their virtual ledger system, FamZoo provides the tools necessary to raise financially responsible adults.

Key Features

  • Family Prepaid Cards: Give kids their own cards with parent-controlled spending limits and real-time activity alerts.
  • Automated Allowance: Set it and forget it with scheduled transfers that move money from the "parent bank" to kids' accounts.
  • Chore Tracking: Create checklists and tie financial rewards to the completion of household tasks and responsibilities.
  • Parent-Paid Interest: Encourage long-term saving by setting a custom interest rate that you pay into your child's savings bucket.
  • Expense Sharing: Teach accountability by having older kids contribute to their own cell phone bills or subscription costs automatically.
  • Savings Goals: Kids can create visual goals for big-ticket items and track their progress through their own dashboard.
  • Educational Giving: Dedicated "Giving" buckets help children understand the importance of charitable donations from an early age.

Who Should Use FamZoo?

FamZoo is the ideal choice for the "mentoring parent"—someone who believes that financial literacy is a critical life skill that should be practiced early. It is perfect for families who want to transition away from messy cash allowances toward a structured, digital-first approach. While standard banking apps are for adults, FamZoo provides unrivaled educational tools and parental oversight for anyone dedicated to raising the next generation of savvy spenders and disciplined savers.
