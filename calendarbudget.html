What is CalendarBudget?

CalendarBudget is a personal finance tool that breaks away from traditional list-based budgeting. Instead of showing you what happened in the past, it uses a familiar calendar interface to show you exactly where your balance will be tomorrow, next month, or even years from now. By treating your budget like your schedule, it makes financial planning intuitive and visual.

Calendar-Based View

See your income, expenses, and account balances mapped directly onto a calendar. If you can use a digital calendar, you can manage your money.

Cash Flow Forecasting

Predict your account balance up to 20 years into the future. Identify potential shortfalls or "savings windows" before they happen.

Automated Reminders

Never miss a bill again. Set up recurring transactions and receive alerts to ensure you always have funds ready for upcoming obligations.

Multi-Device Sync

Access your budget on the go. CalendarBudget stays perfectly in sync across its web browser version and mobile apps for iOS and Android.

Who Should Use CalendarBudget?

CalendarBudget is ideal for visual learners and anyone who finds traditional spreadsheets or list-based apps confusing. It is particularly powerful for people who struggle with timing—knowing when a bill hits relative to a paycheck—and for those who want to see the long-term impact of today's spending decisions on their future wealth.
