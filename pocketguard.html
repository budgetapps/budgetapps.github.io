About PocketGuard

PocketGuard is a powerful personal finance app that helps you track your spending, manage your budget, and save money effortlessly. With its innovative "In My Pocket" feature, you can see exactly how much you have available to spend after accounting for bills, goals, and necessities.

The app automatically categorizes your transactions, identifies recurring subscriptions, and provides personalized insights to help you make smarter financial decisions. PocketGuard's clean interface and intuitive design make it easy for anyone to take control of their finances.

Key Features

  • In My Pocket: See your available spending money after bills and savings
  • Automatic Categorization: Transactions are automatically sorted into categories
  • Subscription Tracking: Identify and manage recurring payments
  • Bill Negotiation: Get help lowering your bills
  • Savings Goals: Set and track your financial objectives
  • Bank-Level Security: 256-bit SSL encryption protects your data
  • Multi-Account Support: Connect all your financial accounts in one place
  • Spending Insights: Detailed analytics on your spending patterns

Who Should Use PocketGuard?

PocketGuard is perfect for individuals who want a simple, straightforward way to manage their money. It's especially useful for people who struggle with overspending or want to see their available spending money at a glance. The app is great for beginners and those who prefer automation over manual budget entry.
