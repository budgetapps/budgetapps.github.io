About Copilot Money

Copilot Money is a premium personal finance app built specifically for the Apple ecosystem. It combines a stunning, high-performance interface with powerful AI that learns your spending habits to provide incredibly accurate categorization and insights.

Designed to be your financial co-pilot, the app focuses on clarity and automation. It aggregates all your financial accounts, including bank accounts, credit cards, investments, and even crypto, to give you a real-time, comprehensive view of your net worth and spending power.

Key Features

  • Intelligence-Driven Categorization: Advanced AI that learns and adapts to how you spend
  • Beautiful Dashboard: High-quality visualizations designed natively for iPhone, iPad, and Mac
  • Real-Time Net Worth: Track your total wealth across all assets and liabilities automatically
  • Subscription Monitoring: Easily identify, track, and manage recurring bills and subscriptions
  • Investment Tracking: Comprehensive views of your portfolio performance and asset allocation
  • Customizable Budgets: Create flexible budgets that make sense for your lifestyle
  • Amazon & Venmo Integration: Special integrations to help you categorize specific itemized purchases
  • Privacy Focused: Ad-free experience with bank-level security and no selling of your data
