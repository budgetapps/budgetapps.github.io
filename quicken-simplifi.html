About Quicken Simplifi

Quicken Simplifi is a modern, ad-free personal finance app designed to give you a comprehensive view of your entire financial life. Developed by the makers of Quicken, Simplifi offers a streamlined experience that balances powerful depth with a clean, user-friendly interface.

The app provides a real-time view of your bank accounts, credit cards, investments, and loans. By automatically categorizing transactions and identifying your recurring bills, Simplifi helps you see exactly where your money goes and how much you have left to spend each month through its dynamic "Spending Plan."

Key Features

  • Personalized Spending Plan: A flexible budget that adapts to your actual income and spending
  • Net Worth Tracking: Monitor your total financial health across all assets and liabilities
  • Investment Tracking: Keep an eye on your portfolio performance and holdings
  • Watchlist Tracking: Set limits on specific categories or tags to prevent overspending
  • Savings Goals: Create multiple goals and track your progress automatically
  • Automatic Categorization: Advanced AI sorts transactions and learns your habits
  • Custom Reports: Detailed visual analytics for income, spending, and savings
  • Ad-Free Experience: A clean interface focused entirely on your financial data

Who Should Use Quicken Simplifi?

Quicken Simplifi is ideal for users who want a high-level overview of their finances without the complexity of traditional desktop software. It’s perfect for those who want to track net worth and investments alongside daily spending. If you value a clean, ad-free interface and want automated insights into your financial health, Simplifi is an excellent choice.
