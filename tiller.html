About Tiller

Tiller is the only service that automatically feeds your daily spending, income, and balances into Google Sheets and Microsoft Excel. It is designed for those who want the ultimate flexibility of a spreadsheet without the hassle of manual data entry or CSV downloads.

With Tiller, you aren't forced into a specific budgeting philosophy. Whether you prefer Zero-Based Budgeting, the 50/30/20 rule, or your own custom system, Tiller provides the foundation by keeping your data accurate and up-to-date. It combines bank-grade security with the privacy of knowing your data stays within your own private spreadsheets.

Key Features

  • Automated Data Feeds: Transactions and balances updated daily in your sheets
  • Google Sheets & Excel: Use the spreadsheet platform you prefer
  • Foundation Template: Get started quickly with a powerful, pre-built budgeting template
  • Auto-Categorization: Create custom rules to sort your transactions automatically
  • Multi-Account Support: Connect accounts from over 21,000 financial institutions
  • Custom Reports: Build your own charts and dashboards to visualize your net worth
  • Privacy & Security: 256-bit AES encryption; Tiller never sees your bank login credentials
  • Daily Email Summaries: Stay informed with a daily digest of your latest activity
