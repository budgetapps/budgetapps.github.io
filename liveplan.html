About LivePlan

LivePlan is a premium financial planning ecosystem designed for entrepreneurs and individuals who need a bridge between high-level strategy and daily budgeting. While many tools focus solely on tracking past spending, LivePlan emphasizes the future. It provides a structured framework for building professional business plans and detailed personal financial forecasts, ensuring that every dollar spent aligns with a larger objective.

The platform is renowned for its "lean planning" methodology, which allows users to quickly test financial assumptions and see how different scenarios—like hiring a new employee or taking out a loan—will impact their cash flow. By combining robust data analysis with an easy-to-use interface, LivePlan transforms complex financial modeling into a manageable, even inspiring, process.

Key Features

  • Step-by-Step Planning: Guided prompts to help you write a comprehensive business or personal financial plan without starting from a blank page.
  • Financial Forecasting: Automatic generation of Profit & Loss statements, Balance Sheets, and Cash Flow projections based on simple inputs.
  • Pitch Deck Builder: Tools to create visual, data-driven presentations for investors, lenders, or personal stakeholders.
  • Live Dashboard: Sync with accounting software to compare your actual performance against your planned budget in real-time.
  • Scenario Modeling: "What-if" tools to explore how different financial decisions will affect your long-term bank balance.
  • Benchmark Data: Compare your financial health and growth metrics against industry averages to see how you stack up.
  • Collaboration Tools: Securely invite partners or advisors to review your plan and contribute to your financial strategy.

Who Should Use LivePlan?

LivePlan is the ideal choice for the "visionary builder"—the entrepreneur starting a new venture or the individual treating their personal wealth like a business. It is perfect for those who need to present their financial health to others or those who require high-level projections to make confident investment decisions. While basic apps handle expense logs, LivePlan provides unrivaled strategic depth and professional modeling for anyone serious about building a sustainable and profitable financial future.
